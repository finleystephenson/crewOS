How it works

  1. Tell us about the job. Talk it out, type it up, or paste your site notes — whichever's fastest. Send it as a reply to our onboarding email.
  2. We turn it into a quote in your voice. A branded HTML quote in your pricing, your trade language, your terms. Drafted in ~8 minutes and emailed back to you for review before it goes to the customer.
  3. We chase it for you. Polite email follow-ups until they book the job, ask a question, or formally say no. You stop forgetting the ones that went quiet.

Common questions

Are you using ChatGPT under the hood?

Frontier-model AI, yes — but the moat isn't the model, it's the trade-specific quote templates, the UK paperwork (CIS, VAT, gas-safe references, NICEIC certificates), and the follow-up sequence we run for you. A generic chatbot won't write a quote your customers will pay for.

What do you do with my customer data?

It stays in your account. We don't sell it. We don't use it to train models. We use it to run the follow-up sequence for the job that's actively quoted. UK-hosted infrastructure.

Will it write a quote that's actually accurate?

Only if you tell it your real prices in the onboarding (10 minutes). It quotes in your pricing voice, not a generic one. You always see and approve the quote before it goes out — we don't auto-send anything to customers without your sign-off.

I do agricultural / electrical / plumbing / boiler / brickwork — does it work for my trade?

v0 supports general builder, electrical install, plumbing & heating, groundworks, and bricklaying templates. If your trade isn't on the list, get on the pilot and tell us — we'll build a template for you inside the 14 days.

Wait — is this built by AI?

Yes, transparently. Crew OS was founded by Finley Stephenson and is operated day-to-day by Claude, an AI agent. The product itself (your quote, your follow-ups, your customer comms) is real software the AI built. Reply to any email to reach a human — Finley reads escalations.

What's the catch with the free pilot?

The catch is honest: we want feedback. We'll ask you for a 10-minute call at the end of the 14 days and a one-paragraph written testimonial if it worked. That's it.
